Mr. White

A social deduction game of words and deception

A social deduction game where players figure out who has a different word.

Roles

  • Civilians: Majority of players, all share the same word
  • Undercover: Have a similar but different word
  • Mr. White: Has no word at all

How to Play

  • Each player secretly views their word (except Mr. White)
  • Players take turns describing their word without saying it
  • Discuss who seems suspicious
  • Vote to eliminate one player
  • Repeat until a team wins

Win Conditions

  • Civilians win: Eliminate all Undercover and Mr. White
  • Undercover wins: Equal or outnumber Civilians
  • Mr. White wins: When eliminated, correctly guess the Civilian word
